Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Total Cash Home Buyers, LLC (“Total Cash Home Buyers,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you visit or use our investor portal at portal.totalchb.com and any related forms, pages, and services (collectively, the “Site”). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly and information collected automatically when you use the Site.

Information you provide

  • Contact and identity details: name, email address, and phone/mobile number.
  • Access credentials: property data access keys and one-time verification codes (OTPs) used to log in.
  • Transaction details: property of interest, requested showing times, offer amounts, and related messages you send us.
  • Communications: the content of forms you submit and any correspondence with us.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages viewed, referring pages, and timestamps.
  • Consent records: the date, time, and version of any consent you give (including text-message opt-in), which we retain as proof of consent.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see Section 5.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Verify your identity and provide secure access to property data on the Site (including sending one-time login codes).
  • Deliver property data access keys and respond to your requests.
  • Schedule and confirm property showings and manage appointments.
  • Process, evaluate, and respond to offers you submit.
  • Send transactional and, where you have opted in, marketing text messages and emails.
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Site.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

3. Text messaging (SMS/MMS)

If you provide your mobile number and check the applicable consent box on a form, you agree to receive text messages from Total Cash Home Buyers, LLC. We offer two separate, optional programs:

  • Transactional (non-marketing) messages — for example, login/verification codes, property data access keys, showing scheduling and reminders, and offer status updates.
  • Marketing messages — for example, special offers, discounts, new property listings, and service updates.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, or reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. You may also revoke consent through any other reasonable method, including by emailing info@totalchb.com or calling (888) 988-9150. After you opt out, we may send a single confirmation message and will stop sending the applicable messages. Consent to marketing messages is not a condition of buying any property or using the Site. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Mobile information sharing. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All of the data-sharing categories described in this Policy exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties. We will only share mobile opt-in data with service providers strictly to the extent necessary to deliver the messages you requested (for example, our messaging platform), and never for their own marketing.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. Subject to the mobile-information limits in Section 3, we may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: vendors that perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, email/SMS delivery, identity verification, analytics, and customer support, who are bound to use the information only to provide services to us.
  • Professional advisors: attorneys, accountants, title companies, and others as needed to complete a transaction you initiate.
  • Legal and safety: when required by law, subpoena, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Total Cash Home Buyers, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to the protections in this Policy.

5. Cookies & tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember preferences, secure the Site, and understand how the Site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect Site functionality. We honor recognized opt-out preference signals where required by law.

6. Data retention

We keep personal information for as long as needed to provide the Site and for legitimate business and legal purposes. We retain records of text-message consent and opt-out requests for at least five (5) years to comply with applicable regulations.

7. Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, to opt out of certain sharing, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To make a request, email info@totalchb.com or call (888) 988-9150. We will verify your request as required by law before responding.

9. Children’s privacy

The Site is intended for adults (18 years or older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Third-party links

The Site may link to third-party websites or services that we do not control. This Policy does not apply to those sites; please review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes may be communicated through the Site or other reasonable means.
